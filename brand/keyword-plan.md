## Phase 3: Web Search Validation - SERP Analysis

### Key SERP Findings

**"rock paper scissors online"**

- Top results: rpsgame.org, bloob.io, online-stopwatch.com, rpsls.net
- SERP quality: Mixed. Thin pages. No strong editorial content. rpsgame.org ranks #1 with minimal copy.
- Gap: Zero content explaining WHY to use one game vs another. No comparison. No trust signals beyond "free."
- PAA likely: "where can I play RPS online?", "can you play RPS against someone online?"

**"rock paper scissors online with friends"**

- Top results: rpsgame.org, bloob.io, rpsmulti.com, rpsonline.app, roshambo.me
- SERP quality: 8-10 competing domains, all thin game pages, no editorial/guide content
- Gap: Nobody ranks a guide-style "how to challenge a friend to RPS" piece. Big PAA opportunity.

**"rock paper scissors multiplayer"**

- Heavily saturated but weak content. bloob.io, rpsmulti.com dominate.
- Gap: No page explains the "share a link, play instantly" workflow in detail.

**"rock paper scissors vs computer"**

- Less saturated. essentially.net, culinaryschools.org, rpsgame.org rank
- Gap: No page benchmarks difficulty, no page explains the AI strategy angle

**"how to win rock paper scissors psychology"**

- Strong editorial competition: MIT Press Reader, Psychology Today, MIT Technology Review
- Gap: No game site links winning strategy content back to their actual game. Big opportunity.
- PAA found: "Is rock paper scissors really random?", "What move wins most in RPS?", "Can you beat the computer at RPS?"

**"settle a bet online"**

- Near zero rock-paper-scissors results. Decision tools dominate (coin flippers, wheel spinners).
- Gap: ZERO RPS sites positioned as decision tools. Complete white space.

**"coin flip with friend online"**

- Dominated by flipsimu.com, tosstogether.app, justflipacoin.com
- Gap: Rock paper scissors as a "more fun coin flip" angle is completely absent

**"best of 3 rock paper scissors"**

- Low competition, mostly forum posts and random pages
- Gap: No definitive "best of 3" guide from any RPS game site

**"rock paper scissors long distance relationship"**

- Low competition! ldrdate.ca ranks, TikTok content present
- Gap: No RPS game site is targeting this use case

**"rock paper scissors icebreaker virtual meeting"**

- team building blogs dominate but no RPS game site ranks here
- Gap: Massive opportunity for a "use RPS as a virtual meeting icebreaker" page

## GEO (AI Recommendation) Strategy

To appear in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews, content must be:

1. **Definitively answerable** - clear H2s that answer specific questions
2. **Structured for extraction** - short answer paragraphs (2-3 sentences), then elaboration
3. **Entity-rich** - mention: rock paper scissors, RPS, roshambo, janken, simultaneous reveal, best of 3, no account required, real-time, browser-based
4. **Use-case explicit** - name scenarios AI would recommend us for: settling disputes, virtual meetings, long distance play, remote teams, quick decisions
