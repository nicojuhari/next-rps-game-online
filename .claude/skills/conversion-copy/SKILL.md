# Conversion Copywriting

Write clear, simple, and high-converting copy.

---

## When to Apply

- Landing pages  
- Website copy  
- Product descriptions  

---

## Core Principles

- Clarity over creativity  
- Focus on benefits, not features  
- Remove friction  

---

## Rules

### 1. Value Proposition

- Clearly explain what it is  
- Explain who it’s for  
- Show why it matters  

---

### 2. Structure

- Strong headline  
- Supporting sections  
- Clear call-to-action  

---

### 3. Simplicity

- Avoid complex wording  
- Keep sentences short  
- Remove unnecessary text  

---

## Output Requirements

- Clear structure  
- Strong messaging  
- Action-oriented copy
