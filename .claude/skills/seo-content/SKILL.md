# SEO Content Writing

Create structured articles designed to rank and convert.

---

## When to Apply

- Writing blog posts  
- Targeting keywords  
- Creating website content  

---

## Core Principles

- Match search intent  
- Be useful and clear  
- Avoid filler content  

---

## Rules

### 1. Structure

- Clear title (H1)  
- Logical headings (H2, H3)  
- Well-separated sections  

---

### 2. Content

- Answer the user’s question directly  
- Provide practical value  
- Keep paragraphs readable
- Use simple and clear words, don't use em dashes, short sentences
- Use the words people use and understand  

---

### 3. Optimization

- Use keywords naturally  
- Include FAQ section  
- Optimize for clarity  

---

## Output Requirements

- SEO-friendly structure  
- Clear headings  
- Useful content  
- FAQ section
