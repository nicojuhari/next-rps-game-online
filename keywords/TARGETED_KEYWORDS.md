## Keywords to Avoid (wrong audience)

These appear in the data but bring zero-value traffic for a game site:

- `rock paper scissors book` / `alice feeney` — novel, not a game
- `rock paper scissors salon` / `hairdresser` — local business
- `strip rock paper scissors` — different kind of site
- `rock paper scissors yellow dress` — viral video
- `rpg game online` — completely different genre (Role Playing Game)
- `rts game online` — different genre (Real-Time Strategy)
- `rock paper scissors nickelodeon` — TV show
- `circle k rock paper scissors` — gas station promotion

## Notes on AI Search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overview)

AI search tends to cite pages that:
- Directly answer a specific question
- Have clear structure (headers, lists)
- Are fast and mobile-friendly (this site is)

Best angles for AI citations:
- "How to win rock paper scissors" — clear tips with evidence
- "Rock paper scissors rules" — simple, factual
- "What is the most common throw in rock paper scissors" — specific, citable stat
- "How does rock paper scissors work online" — directly describes the site's use case
