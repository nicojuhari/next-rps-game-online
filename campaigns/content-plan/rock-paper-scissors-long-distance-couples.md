## Recommended Outline

### H1: Rock Paper Scissors for Long-Distance Couples - Play Together in Seconds, No App Needed

**Opening (2-3 sentences):** You're on a call with your partner. The moment calls for a friendly competition. Rock paper scissors is perfect - except texting or video call counting leads to someone always seeing the other's move first. Here's how to do it fairly, instantly, from anywhere.

### H2: Why Rock Paper Scissors Is Perfect for Long-Distance Couples

- Instant - no setup, no waiting to "install something"
- Zero pressure - if one of you loses at 11pm you can just blame bad luck
- Decisions - use it to decide who picks the next movie, show, restaurant for your next in-person date
- Slightly competitive - creates a moment of drama without any stakes
- Works on any device - phone, laptop, tablet
- ~200 words

### H2: The Problem With Playing on FaceTime or Video Call

_(Addresses the PAA directly)_

- The classic "1-2-3 shoot" on camera has a lag problem
- One player reveals a half-second before the other - the second player can adjust
- This ruins the simultaneous reveal that makes RPS fair
- Online RPS fixes this: both picks are locked in before either player sees the result
- ~150 words

### H2: How to Play Rock Paper Scissors Online With Your Partner (Step by Step)

1. One partner opens the 2-player game - takes 5 seconds
2. Copy the game link - one tap
3. Send it to your partner over text, Discord, WhatsApp, wherever
4. Partner opens it on their device - any device, any browser
5. Both pick at the same time - simultaneous reveal, no peeking possible
6. Winner wins, loser owes a dare or gets to choose the next Netflix show

Total time from idea to playing: under 60 seconds.
~200 words

### H2: Fun Ways to Use Rock Paper Scissors in Your Long-Distance Relationship

#### H3: The Decision Game

- Use best of 3 to decide: who texts first tomorrow, who picks the movie for your next virtual date, who books the next visit
- Adds game theory to relationship decisions - more fun than "you decide"

#### H3: The Dare Edition

- Loser of each round has to do a small dare: share an embarrassing photo, do a silly dance on camera, send a compliment
- Turns a 3-round game into 15 minutes of entertainment

#### H3: The Running Scoreboard

- Keep a running tally across multiple sessions
- After 10 rounds, the loser owes something (a letter, a care package, planning the next date)

#### H3: The Pre-Date Warmup

- Before your virtual date night, play best of 3 to decide who picks the activity
- Gets you both playful and present before the actual date

~250 words across all H3s

### H2: Can You Play on Any Device?

_(Addresses PAA about phones)_

- Yes - browser-based, no app needed
- Works on iOS and Android phones
- Partner doesn't need to install anything
- ~80 words

### H2: Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do we both need accounts?**
A: No. No accounts, no email, no password. Open the link and play.

**Q: What if we're in different time zones and one of us is on mobile?**
A: Works on any device, any browser, from any location worldwide.

**Q: Is there a way to play more than one round?**
A: Yes - the game plays best of 3. You can play as many sessions as you want.

**Q: What if we want to play more than rock paper scissors?**
A: Try a best-of-5 session. Or play multiple rounds across the week and keep a score.
