## Competitor Analysis

### 1. rpsgame.org

- **Title:** Rock Paper Scissors - Play Online with Friends
- **Why it ranks:** Aged domain with exact-match brand feel, strong brand recognition, broad link profile
- **Gaps:** No meta description, sparse content, no structured data, shallow page depth

### 2. bloob.io/rps

- **Title:** Play Rock Paper Scissors Online | Free Multiplayer RPS Game
- **H1:** "Experience Customizable Rock Paper Scissors: Play Variations with Friends or Bots Online"
- **Why it ranks:** Best content depth among game-only sites - FAQ section, multiple modes (Race, Survival, Best of Series), 2–4 players, bots, mobile-optimized
- **Gaps:** No meta description, no structured data

### 3. wrpsa.com (World RPS Association)

- **Why it ranks:** Dominant authority site. Full editorial strategy: tournament coverage, world rankings, strategy guides, arcade, 10+ blog articles targeting every long-tail variation. The topical authority blueprint.
- **Gaps:** Some pages 403; arcade game itself is limited vs dedicated game sites

### 4. rockpaperscissoronline.com

- **Title:** Play Rock Paper Scissors Online Free | AI Opponent & Strategy Guide
- **H1:** Rock Paper Scissors Online
- **H2s:** History, Advanced Strategy, Variations, How to Play Online, Features, FAQ
- **Why it ranks:** Adaptive AI (Markov chain), achievements, daily leaderboard + full FAQ, history, strategy guide all on one page. Multiple keyword intents on one URL.
- **Gaps:** No meta description, no structured data

### 5. rpschampion.com

- **Title:** Play Rock Paper Scissors Online Multiplayer
- **H1:** Play Rock Paper Scissors with Friends Online
- **H2s:** Quick Guide, How to Play with Friends, RPS Around the World, How to Play (rules), FAQ, Strategy Guide
- **Why it ranks:** 6-digit room codes, no-signup, best-of-5, AI practice mode, strategy guide, FAQ, world variations - well-rounded content + game
- **Gaps:** No meta description, no structured data

### 6. coolmathgames.com/0-rock-paper-scissors

- **Why it ranks:** Pure domain authority. 78,333+ user ratings, massive internal linking, millions of users. The RPS page itself is thin; ranks on domain muscle alone.
- **Gaps:** Shallow standalone content, old Flash issues

### 7. roshambo.me

- **Title:** Challenge a Friend | Roshambo (Rock-Paper-Scissors)
- **Why it ranks:** Targets "roshambo" variant keyword, offers RPS + RPSLS, multiple match formats, game history/leaderboard
- **Gaps:** No meta description, no structured data, niche domain limits broader ranking

## Why Competitors Rank - Key Patterns

1. **Keyword in title + H1** - Every top site has "Rock Paper Scissors" + "Online" in both. Table stakes.
2. **Content depth on game page** - Winners have history, strategy guide, FAQ, and "How to Play" sections below the game UI.
3. **FAQ sections** - Captures "People Also Ask" boxes and voice search queries.
4. **Topical authority (WRPSA model)** - The site that owns the topic holistically (rules, history, tournaments, strategy, online play, variants) ranks across all intent types.
5. **Domain authority by association (Coolmath model)** - High-DA umbrella domains lift thin pages. Can't replicate but can compete on content quality.
6. **Structured data gap** - Zero competitors use `VideoGame` schema. Uncontested opportunity.
7. **Meta description gap** - Most competitors have none. A good meta description improves CTR immediately.
8. **Real-time multiplayer differentiator** - "Rock paper scissors with friends online" surfaces a smaller competitive pool. This is our strongest differentiation angle.
9. **Mobile-first signals** - bloob.io explicitly markets mobile; mobile-first indexing means this matters directly.

## Phase 1 - Foundation (do first, highest ROI)

- [ ] Add meta descriptions to all pages
- [ ] Add canonical tags
- [ ] Add `og:title`, `og:description`, `og:image` for social previews
- [ ] Add `VideoGame` JSON-LD structured data to both game pages (see schema below)
- [ ] Add a content section + FAQ below each game UI (~300–500 words)
- [ ] Create `sitemap.xml`

### VideoGame JSON-LD Schema

```json
{
    "@context": "https://schema.org",
    "@type": "VideoGame",
    "name": "Rock Paper Scissors Online",
    "description": "Play Rock Paper Scissors online for free. Single-player vs computer or real-time multiplayer with friends. No download, no signup.",
    "url": "https://yoursite.com",
    "playMode": ["MultiPlayer", "SinglePlayer"],
    "gamePlatform": "Browser",
    "applicationCategory": "Game",
    "offers": {
        "@type": "Offer",
        "price": "0",
        "priceCurrency": "USD"
    }
}
```
